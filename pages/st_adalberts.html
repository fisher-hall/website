YGTSA - "Ya Gotta Tutor St Adalbert's"



St. Adalbert's Catholic School

Fisher hall has been blessed with the opportunity to give back to the South Bend community by supporting St. Adalbert's Catholic School. Every year, we make a donation to St. Adalbert's with funds raised from the Fisher Regatta, and we also have weekly tutoring at St. Adalbert's.
